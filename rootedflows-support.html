Frequently asked

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple Account. On your iPhone, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, choose Rooted Flows, and you can change or cancel your plan there. You can also turn off auto-renew the same way.

How do I restore a purchase?

In the app, open Settings → Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and your associated data and can't be undone.

The barcode scanner isn't reading a product.

The scanner needs camera access and good lighting. If a product isn't found, you can enter it manually. Product information comes from the Open Food Facts and Open Beauty Facts databases, so some items may not be listed yet.

A note on what Rooted Flows is

Rooted Flows is a wellness and educational app. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. Always talk to a qualified healthcare provider about your health.
