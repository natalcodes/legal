Privacy Policy

Rooted Flows · Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Rooted Flows ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Rooted Flows mobile application (Bundle ID: com.rootedflows.rootedflows).

Sensitive health data notice: Rooted Flows collects and processes reproductive health information, including menstrual cycle data, period dates, symptoms, and related wellness data. We treat this information with heightened care. Please read this policy carefully so you understand exactly what is collected, where it is stored, and your control over it.

By using Rooted Flows, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Name and email address.
  • Cycle and period data: Period start and end dates, cycle length, flow intensity, and other cycle-related entries you log.
  • Symptoms and wellness logs: Symptoms, mood, energy, and other wellness data you choose to record.
  • Scanned product data: When you use the Scanner feature, we send the product barcode or ingredient query to public databases (Open Food Facts, Open Beauty Facts, PubChem) to look up information. We do not transmit your personal account information with these queries.

1.2 HealthKit data

HealthKit disclosure: With your explicit permission, Rooted Flows reads the following data from Apple HealthKit: menstrual cycle / period data, sleep analysis, heart rate variability (HRV), and active energy. With your permission, the App may also write the following data back to HealthKit: period dates and recorded symptoms. HealthKit data is processed on your device and is only synced to our servers if you have enabled cloud backup; otherwise it stays on your device.

You can grant or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Rooted Flows.

Important: Data obtained from HealthKit is never used for advertising, marketing, or sold to third parties. HealthKit data is not shared with our analytics provider (Mixpanel) or any other third party except as strictly required to provide a feature you have requested.

1.3 Information collected automatically

  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned by Supabase Auth.
  • Usage analytics: Information about how you interact with the App, including screens viewed and features used. Collected via Mixpanel. We do not include cycle/period data, symptom content, or HealthKit data in analytics events.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: Used to improve stability.
  • Subscription status: Managed via RevenueCat.

1.4 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect precise device location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos library (except via the Scanner camera with your permission), microphone, or messages.
  • We do not collect financial information — payments are handled by Apple In-App Purchase.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide cycle tracking, predictions, and wellness insights.
  • To allow you to log and review symptoms, sleep, HRV, and active energy.
  • To sync your data across your devices (if you enable cloud sync).
  • To look up product safety and ingredient information when you use the Scanner.
  • To improve App performance, fix bugs, and understand which features are useful.
  • To manage subscriptions and respond to support requests.

3. Reproductive Health Data — Special Protections

We recognize that reproductive health data is highly sensitive. We apply the following protections:

  • No advertising use: Your cycle, period, and symptom data is never used for advertising or shared with advertising networks.
  • No sale of data: We do not sell your personal information to anyone, ever.
  • No law-enforcement disclosure without legal process: We will not voluntarily disclose your reproductive health data. We will only respond to valid legal process (such as a court order or subpoena) and will, where lawful, provide notice to you so you can challenge it.
  • Local-first option: If you turn off cloud sync, your cycle and symptom data remains on your device only.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all reproductive health data associated with your account is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Encryption: Cycle data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest on Supabase infrastructure.

5. Data Storage and Security

Account data and (if you enable cloud sync) cycle/symptom data are stored on Supabase infrastructure (hosted on AWS) with encryption in transit and at rest. HealthKit data is stored on your device by Apple and is governed by Apple's HealthKit privacy framework. We restrict internal access to user data to what is necessary to operate the App.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and cycle data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our active systems within 30 days, with backup purges completed within 90 days. Data written to HealthKit remains under your control in the Health app even after account deletion.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correction: View and edit any of your data inside the App.
  • Export: Export your cycle and symptom data from within App settings.
  • Account deletion: Delete your account in App settings, or email support@rootedflows.com.
  • HealthKit permissions: Granted or revoked in iOS Settings → Health.
  • Analytics opt-out: Disable in App settings.
  • Cloud sync opt-out: Disable cloud sync in App settings to keep all cycle data local to your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Rooted Flows is rated 17+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

9. International Users

Rooted Flows is not distributed in the European Union and is not designed to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the personal data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of any "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information. Reproductive health data is treated as sensitive personal information under California law and is not used for purposes beyond providing the App's features. To exercise your rights, contact support@rootedflows.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your reproductive health data, contact us at support@rootedflows.com.
