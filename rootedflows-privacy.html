Rooted Flows ("Rooted Flows," "we," "us") is a wellness app for understanding your menstrual cycle, perimenopause, and menopause. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why, how we handle it, and the choices you have. We've tried to keep it plain.

Rooted Flows is a wellness and educational app, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. See our Terms of Service.

1. Who handles your data

Rooted Flows is operated by [LEGAL ENTITY / OWNER NAME], [LOCATION]. For any privacy question or request, contact us at support@rootedflows.com.

2. What we collect and why

We only collect what the app needs to work for you. We do not buy data about you, and we do not run advertising or third-party tracking.

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and sign in to your account. Authentication is handled by Supabase.
  • Cycle and wellness entries you choose to log — period and cycle dates, flow intensity, cycle and period length, symptoms, mood, and any notes you write. We use these to show you where you are in your cycle or season and to personalize the recipes and practices we suggest.
  • Your profile settings — your journey stage (cycling, perimenopausal, or menopausal), birth-control method and pack rhythm (if you enter them), and dietary preferences or allergens. These tailor what the app shows you.
  • Onboarding answers — the responses you give when setting up the app, used to set your starting profile.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Premium subscription. Purchases are made through Apple's App Store; we use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We never receive or store your payment-card details — Apple handles payment.
  • Ingredient scanner — when you scan a product barcode, that barcode is sent to the Open Food Facts and Open Beauty Facts databases to look up product and ingredient information. We don't attach your identity to those lookups.
  • Basic technical data — limited information needed to operate the app reliably (for example, secure connections to our servers). We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

What we do not collect: we do not connect to Apple HealthKit, we do not use advertising identifiers, and we do not include third-party trackers in the app.

3. How your data is stored and protected

Your account and entries are stored with Supabase, our hosting and database provider, on servers located in [SUPABASE DATA REGION]. Data is encrypted in transit. Access is restricted by row-level security so that your entries are accessible only to your account.

4. Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app, and only as needed:

  • Supabase — secure hosting, authentication, and database storage.
  • Apple — processes your subscription purchase and payment.
  • RevenueCat — manages your subscription status (using an anonymous identifier).
  • Open Food Facts / Open Beauty Facts — receive scanned barcodes to return product information (data licensed under the Open Database License).

We do not sell your personal or health data, and we do not share it for advertising. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our users.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access and edit — you can view and change your cycle data, profile, and preferences directly in the app at any time.
  • Delete your account and data — in the app, go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and your associated personal data and cannot be undone.
  • Request a copy — to request a copy of your data, email support@rootedflows.com.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, deletion, or portability). Contact us and we'll help.

6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your associated personal data is deleted.
