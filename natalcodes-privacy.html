Privacy Policy

Natalcodes · Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Natalcodes ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Natalcodes mobile application (Bundle ID: com.natalcodes.natalcodes). The App is operated by Pepper Nguyen as an individual developer.

By using Natalcodes, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Your name and email address, used to create and manage your account.
  • Birth data: Your date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth. This information is required to generate astrological natal charts and readings — it is the core input for the App's primary feature.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account by our authentication provider (Supabase Auth).
  • Usage analytics: Information about how you interact with the App, including screens viewed, features used, and session duration. Collected via Mixpanel.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: Technical information about app crashes and errors, used solely to improve stability and fix bugs.
  • Subscription status: Information about your subscription state (active, expired, trial), managed via RevenueCat.

1.3 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect precise device location (GPS).
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • We do not collect financial information — all payments are processed by Apple via In-App Purchase.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create your account and authenticate you.
  • To generate astrological readings, natal charts, and personalized content based on your birth data.
  • To process your birth location into geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) using the Google Maps Geocoding API, which is required for accurate astrological calculations.
  • To improve App performance, fix bugs, and understand which features are useful.
  • To manage subscriptions and entitlements.
  • To respond to your support requests.

3. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

Important: Readings are generated by OpenAI GPT-4o. Your birth data and prompts may be sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI does not use API data for training.

When you request a reading, the App sends a prompt to OpenAI's API. This prompt may include your birth data (date, time, place) and the specific astrological context being interpreted. AI-generated content is for entertainment and reflection only and should not be relied upon for important life decisions.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (hosted on AWS) with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We restrict access to user data to what is necessary to operate the App. While we apply reasonable safeguards, no system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and birth data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our active systems within 30 days. Backup copies are purged on a rolling basis within 90 days. Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained indefinitely.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correction: You can view and update your name, email, and birth data at any time inside the App.
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account from within the App settings, or by emailing us at support@natalcodes.com. Deletion is permanent.
  • Analytics opt-out: You can disable analytics tracking in the App's settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Natalcodes is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Users

Natalcodes is not distributed in the European Union and is not designed to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where our servers and providers are located.

10. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the personal data we collect, to request deletion of your data, and to opt out of any "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@natalcodes.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email.
